Sapporo Classic Lager

One of Japan’s oldest bottled beers.

More often seen on bar counters than at home.

Heavier than modern lagers, and intentionally unchanged.

It endures because it has loyal followers—people who don’t switch once they’ve chosen.

In Japan it’s often called “Akaboshi” (Red Star).

In one iconic film scene, Ken Takakura quietly drinks it after being released from prison.

That same actor is the idol of Momo Ayase in the anime Dan Da Dan.
